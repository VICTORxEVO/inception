Key Features

What Makes Them Special

Every RX 9000 card ships with the full RDNA 4 feature set.

FSR

FSR 4

First AMD GPU with dedicated AI matrix cores to power FidelityFX Super Resolution 4, AMD's machine-learning upscaler for near-native image quality.

AI-PoweredWMMA Cores
RT

3rd-Gen Ray Tracing

64 dedicated Ray Accelerators on the XT model deliver 2× the RT throughput per CU vs RDNA 3, enabling fully path-traced games at 60+ fps with FSR 4.

2× RTBVH Hardware
AV1

Dual AV1 Encode

Two hardware AV1 encoders support simultaneous game capture and streaming at up to 8K60, with near-visually-lossless quality at low bitrates.

8K60Low Bitrate

DisplayPort 2.1a
